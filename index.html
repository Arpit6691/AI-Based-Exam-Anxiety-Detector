AI-Based Exam Anxiety Detector

Detecting Student Exam Stress using Artificial Intelligence

Project Overview

The AI-Based Exam Anxiety Detector is a mental wellness support system designed to identify exam-related anxiety from student-written text such as reflections, feedback, or thoughts before exams.

The system analyzes emotional patterns in text and classifies anxiety into three levels: Low Anxiety, Moderate Anxiety, and High Anxiety.

The goal of this system is to help students, teachers, and educational institutions detect stress early and provide appropriate emotional or academic support.

Anxiety Levels

Low Anxiety 😊

Student feels confident and relaxed about the exam.

Moderate Anxiety 😐

Student feels slightly nervous but still manageable.

High Anxiety 😟

Student feels stressed, worried, or overwhelmed.

Use Case Scenarios

1️⃣ Pre-Exam Anxiety Assessment

Students can write their feelings before an exam. The system analyzes the text and provides instant feedback about their anxiety level along with calming suggestions.

2️⃣ Mental Wellness Monitoring

Educational institutions can analyze overall anxiety trends among students before major exams and arrange counselling sessions, workshops, or academic support programs.
